Information on Summer Workshops

1. Robotics by Children technology workshop: Hands-on learning in a dynamic and engaging environment offers students multiple opportunities to develop and explore their academic knowledge. Robotics and Astronomy are interdisciplinary programs that bring together elements in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math.

  • Elementary Robotics- Focuses on developing a basic interest about Science and familiarity with Science concepts.
  • Intermediate Robotics- Uses Lego Mind storms as a backbone. Students build and program robots in every class using several different type of sensors.

 

2.Craft Workshop :

6 to 9 years: Thai Clay broach, Distress ink stamping envelope, Quilling Fridge Magnet and Pencil holder, Origami Tea bag folding, Gift wrapping bow, Clay crafting animals, Soap craft and Origami shirt card
Material to bring: 2 empty plastic boxes, fevicol, fevi stick, pencil and eraser
9 years and above: Thai clay flowers, Distress ink stamping card, 3-D quilling doll, Quilling teddy keychain, Gift wrapping bow, Miniature quilling roses flower pot, Soap craft and Sospeso art Italian decoupage
Material to bring: Fevicol, 2 empty plastic boxes, thin sharp scissors, newspaper, rough cloth, pencil and eraser. 

 

3 Dance Toddle Feet:

The programme will be conducted by Swara Patel and Deepika Ravindran of  Rythmus Happy Feet. They specialise in teaching children dance with a unique approach of developmentally appropriate music and syllabus. The workshop is a joyful way for children to explore movement through music, develop physical skills, channel energy, stimulate imagination and promote creativity. The workshop includes understanding of rhythm, developing motor skills, co-ordination, flexibility, understanding dance concept, knowing dance vocabulary and much more

 

4. Creative writing:

The programme will enable introduction to creative writing, focus on sub-genres in writing such as Fantasy, Adventure and Autobiography, stimulate creative writing skills through outdoor visits, build imagination and improve vocabulary.

 

5.  Unleash your voice:

The programme focuses on diction and pronunciation, art of elocution, voice modulation, and basics of public speaking.

The above programmes (Creative writing and Unleash your voice) will be conducted by Rima Medhi of Creatiwitty.inc who is a successful Radio Jockey with top Radio Stations in Indi & UAE (Radio Mirchi, HUM FM, Fever 104, Planet Radio City). She is also a voice and theatre artist.

 

6. Summer Dance Trail :

The programme will introduce different genres of dance i.e Hip Hop, Contemporary and Afro Jazz. The workshop will give you an understanding of different styles and health benefits and give you the platform to perform the same and boost your confidence. The 3 day dance workshop will culminate with a presentation by students for each style on the 4th day, where parents and friends can be accompanied by the students. All the facilitators are trained under Shaimak Davar at one year training programme. Leena Pandhare will conduct the Contemporary workshop, which is more of ballet workout contemporary and a routine. Pratik Vaidya will conduct Hip Hop, which has various core workout, house technique and routine. Pooja Yadav will conduct Afro Jazz, which is a blend of African tribal and jazz style and has aggressive and stylised movements together.
