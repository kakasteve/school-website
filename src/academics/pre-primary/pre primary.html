Lets start the school by the Pre-primary section:



All the children at Shishuvan are encouraged to learn new things.


Shishuvan,a school with a wholly new concept allows the children to recognise their inner personality.

The different aspects of pre-primary learning are:
- Learning alphabets and numbers through activities.
- Physical fitness like playing,running,etc.
- Field visits to places like: Bus stop,Railway stations,Post office,etc.
- A.V Sessions of educational videos.
- Mixing with people.
- Forming a picture in their mind about what they have learnt in theat class.
- Learning about plants and animals.

The children learn through different types of activities conducted by the school.
